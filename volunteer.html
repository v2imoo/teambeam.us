Beam Volunteers

Give back beyond the one-off day.

An ongoing, opt-in way for your people to give time and skills — and to share the communities and cultures they care about. Connection that keeps going long after the event ends.

Opt-in · ongoing · employee-led — never an obligation.

What it is

An Impact day plants the seed. This keeps it growing.

A great give-back experience is a moment. Beam Volunteers turns that moment into a habit — a simple, ongoing program that lets people contribute time and skills to causes they choose, on their own terms.

It runs alongside everything else we do, and it gives a team a shared sense of purpose that's hard to build any other way.

"Will this feel like another mandatory thing?"

No. Beam Volunteers is opt-in by design. People take part because they want to, in the causes that matter to them — which is exactly why it builds real belonging instead of quiet resentment.

Opt-in always · genuine, never forced
Ways to take part

Three ways your people can give.

However someone wants to contribute, there's a low-friction path for it.

⏱️

Give time

Hands-on volunteering days and ongoing shifts with vetted local organizations, on or off the clock.

🛠️

Give skills

Skills-based volunteering — mentoring, pro-bono work, and expertise donated where it's needed most.

🌍

Share your community

Bring a cause or community you care about to the team, and rally support around it.

Your people, your roots

A place to share what matters to you.

Every team is made of people with their own backgrounds, communities, and traditions. Beam Volunteers gives them a genuine, respectful way to share that — to introduce a cause, a community, or a piece of their culture to the rest of the team.

It's how a team learns about one another for real — and how belonging stops being a slogan and starts being something people feel.
